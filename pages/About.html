About

Who

we

Are

We are a brand based in Bs As. Arg we also believe in rehuse clothing for a better world. We sell used clothing pre-selected and in excellent conditions from Japan, Usa, Europe, Korea, etc. Our prefered style is streetwear and techwear. We believe that one day, there will be no longer the need for people wearing clothes that, as far as we concerned, are 'unfit to be worn'. That is, until people start talking about the 'unfit to be worn' as an unacceptable lifestyle choice. The message from the media has been that in the name of aesthetics, we need to be unhappy. There's a huge focus on the physical appearance... and the emotional. But we’re definitely not a traditional store in the world of fashion, your style is not just going to be the thing you look like and you're going to give the right impression... you keep your voice going.
